Food and Beverage

Our F&B department is a further value add to the services we provide to all our patients. The entire food process adheres to HACCP principles, ISO 22000 Food Safety Management System, and other applicable regulatory requirements. All our patients' dietary requirements are met as advised by our physicians and dietitians only. The production floor and menus are developed with a deep understanding of patient's nutritional needs, clinical requirements, tests, and more recently, cultural and religious requirements in their meals. We understand the need for variety, for excellence in delivery and presentation of star classes Hotel-style meals, and for systems that can respond to changing demands. 24 hours In-room dining services for all our patients and their guardians. Varieties of Dietary menus and meal choices for patients. Personalize, friendly, efficient and professional services. Special orders are accepted and delivered to your bedside and many more.
